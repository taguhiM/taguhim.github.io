Taguhi Mesropyan

PhD Candidate, University of Amsterdam

I am a doctoral researcher at INDElab, University of Amsterdam, supervised by Victoria Degeler and Paul Groth .

I research how complex sensor-rich systems evolve over time, and how we can detect and manage drift in such environments, with a focus on Digital Twins. My work aims to develop a systematic framework for understanding, detecting, and adapting to drift across different components of these systems.

I hold an MSc in Mathematical Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and a BSc in Mathematics from Yerevan State University. I have worked as a data scientist and AI engineer in both industry and R&D.
